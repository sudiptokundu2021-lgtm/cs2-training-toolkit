## 📋 Project Overview

The cs2-training-toolkit provides tools for players to study their performance in Counter-Strike 2. The software uses computer vision to track movement and targets within the game. It helps you analyze your playstyle and shows where you can make improvements. The system runs alongside the game to provide data about your accuracy and reaction times. It simplifies the process of reviewing complex game data by showing clear statistics on your screen.

## 📊 Understanding Your Data

The toolkit displays information using the following categories:

*   Target Acquisition: Measures how fast you move your crosshair toward opponents.
*   Accuracy Percentage: Records the ratio of shots that hit the target versus misses.
*   Reaction Latency: Shows the time in milliseconds between a visual change on screen and your input.
*   Movement Analysis: Tracks your positioning efficiency during competitive matches.
